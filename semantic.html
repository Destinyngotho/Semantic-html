Academy of certified human resource profesionals

am the CEO of Academy of Certified Human Resource Professionals (ACHRP), a leading provider of training, coaching, and audit services for human resource practitioners in Africa. With multiple certifications, including Certified Human Resource Auditor (CHRA), Certified Workplace Counselor (CWC), and Certified Human Resource Business Partner (CHRBP), I have demonstrated my expertise and commitment to the field of human resources management. My academic background includes a Bachelor of Education degree from Moi University, a Post Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management from Kenya Institute of Management, a Post Graduate Certificate in Environmental Impact Assessment & Audit from Africa Nazarene University, and a Certified Human Resource Professional certification from College of Human Resource Management. I am passionate about enhancing the skills, knowledge, and competencies of human resource professionals, as well as promoting ethical and sustainable practices in the workplace. As the CEO of ACHRP, I have led the development and delivery of high-quality courses, workshops, and webinars, reaching over 10,000 participants across 15 countries. I have also supported and empowered hundreds of human resource professionals to achieve excellence and impact in their roles and organizations, through personalized coaching and audit services. My goal is to advance the human resources profession and create positive change in the workplace.
